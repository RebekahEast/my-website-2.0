My Application

Why I want to join Founders and Coders

I've been working with a range of coding languages since June 2022. I think that FAC is the perfect place for me to continue my learning of coding.



Why FAC?

In June 2022, I wanted to make a career change. I had always been interested in tech and coding, but honestly I just assumed it was too late for me. After thinking about it for a while, my friend mentioned she had a friend who would be good for me to meet. We met for a drink and she showed me the code she'd been working on, and how she'd only been doing it for a few years. Turns out she'd going through the FAC programme and couldn't rave about it higher. I was very interested and decided to have a deeper look into the programme. Obviously, I loved the sound of it! The rest is history really.


Why I'm Applying?

I've applied to FAC because I feel that I can learn a wealth of knowledge from the bootcamp. Not only that, but I feel like the people that apply (as I've met a few on line and in person) are the compassionate and inspiring people I would fit in well with. Lastly but not lead, I'm applying because FAC makes everyone feel welcome and included - which is incredibly important!
